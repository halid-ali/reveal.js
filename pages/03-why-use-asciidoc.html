Why use AsciiDoc?

Here are some of the key benefits of using AsciiDoc for documentation:

  • Flexibility:
    easy to read and write, and offers a flexible syntax
  • Extensibility:
    developers and technical writers can create custom macros, etc.
  • Versioning:
    supports versioning
  • Portability:
    can be easily converted to other formats like HTML, PDF, and ePub

AsciiDoc is designed to be easy to read and write, and offers a flexible syntax that can be used to create a wide range of documentation styles.
