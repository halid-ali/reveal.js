Partials

Partials are good for storing snippets of content that you reuse in one or more pages throughout your site.

  • Partial files are stored in a partials directory.
  • A partial’s filename can’t contain spaces because the AsciiDoc include directive doesn’t accept values with spaces.
  • A partial must be referenced by an include directive from a page.
  • Antora doesn’t publish partial files as individual site pages.
